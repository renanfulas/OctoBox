## Current live hypothesis

Reception is the right next expansion because:

1. dashboard already proved the premium grammar
2. finance already proved the first governed expansion
3. reception now tests whether the grammar can survive in a faster and more operational context

The open question is not whether reception should evolve, but how quietly that evolution must be delivered.

Implementation confirmed the following:

1. reception shell and hero can take a richer surface treatment safely
2. intake and payment can carry more command depth than support blocks
3. class-grid and shortcuts benefit from quieter premium support styling
4. the reception surface does not need dashboard-style glow to feel coherent with the premium family
