# Specification

## Problem

The OctoBOX canonical theme is now governed and published, but a few high-visibility areas still expose a legacy premium dialect that conflicts with the new authority.

This shows up as:

- inline style and inline script still dominating key blocks
- `elite-glass-card` and `glass-panel-elite` surviving as parallel surface systems
- advisor narrative and manifesto pieces feeling disconnected from the new shell
- finance and student financial internals still speaking a previous visual language

## Users

- Owner
- Manager
- Reception

## Requirements

### PLI-01

The target legacy islands must feel like part of the same product as the canonical theme.

### PLI-02

Dominant inline presentation debt in the target scope must be removed or reduced to non-sovereign leftovers only.

### PLI-03

`elite-glass-card` and `glass-panel-elite` must no longer act as sovereign theme hosts in the touched areas.

### PLI-04

The advisor narrative and manifesto modal must preserve their product intent while aligning with the canonical theme.

### PLI-05

The finance hero and movement surfaces must preserve clarity and urgency without relying on legacy premium wrappers.

### PLI-06

The student financial workspace must preserve trust and emphasis after premium legacy wrappers are retired.

### PLI-07

Touched areas must keep current behavior stable.

### PLI-08

The cleanup must avoid reopening solved global theme decisions.

### PLI-09

The resulting UI must remain dark, premium, breathable, and adjustable.

## Success

This phase is successful when:

- the touched legacy islands no longer feel like separate products
- dominant inline debt is materially reduced in the target templates
- premium legacy wrappers stop acting as primary theme containers in the touched scope
- the app feels more visually continuous after a short visual review
- no functional regressions are introduced in the touched flows
